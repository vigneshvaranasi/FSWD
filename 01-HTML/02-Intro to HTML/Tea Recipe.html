Why Tea?

Tea offers a peaceful break from the rush, packed with antioxidants to bolster your well-being. It's a gentle energy lift without caffeine's jitters, a simple route to staying hydrated, and a delightful daily ritual for a balanced life.

Making Tea

Preparation Time:2 minutes
Cooking Time:10 minutes
Serves:2 servings (2 cups)

Ingredients

  • cup (250 ml) Milk
  • Tea Powder
  • 1/4 cup (approx. 60 ml) Water
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Boil water in a saucepan.
  2. Add tea powder and sugar.
  3. Stir it well.
  4. Add milk and bring it to boil.
  5. Strain the tea into cups.
  6. Enjoy your tea.
